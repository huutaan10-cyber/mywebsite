About Us

Somali Water Solutions is dedicated to providing sustainable water management services in Somalia. Founded in 2020, we have been working to address water scarcity issues through innovative solutions.

Our team consists of experts in water engineering, environmental science, and community development. We believe in empowering local communities with the tools and knowledge to manage their water resources effectively.
